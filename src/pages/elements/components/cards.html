Cards

Standard card.
The standard card features a gradient from top to bottom and you can add optional highlights to the corners.
Notice card.
The notice card fades to black toward the right, but is held together by a strong border at the bottom.
Solid card.
The solid card has a transparent background without a gradient. It also shows alternative line shaped corner highlights.
